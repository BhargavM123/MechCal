Non-Concurrent Forces

It is defined as the forces which lies in the line of activity but do not intersect or forms a parallel at a common point.

STEP 1

Enter and seperate all the forces and angles in the respective boxes with a comma(,)

STEP 2

All the forces should be coming out of the respective point of contact

STEP 3

Enter angles w.r.t +ve x-axis in anti-clockwise direction in degrees

STEP 4

Enter direction as +1 if force rotates in anti-clockwise dir. w.r.t given point

STEP 4

Enter direction as -1 if force rotates in clockwise dir. w.r.t given point

STEP 4

Enter perpendicular distance of force w.r.t the mentioned point(given point) in meters

FINISH

Click on the "Calculate " button to get the answer!
